Avatar: The Last Airbender

In this section I will introduce the first show in the avatar universe -- Avatar: the Last Airbender. This includes the plot, the main characters, reviews, and where to watch

Plot

In a war-torn world of elemental magic, a young boy reawakens to undertake a dangerous mystic quest to fulfill his destiny as the Avatar, and bring peace to the world. The world is divided into four elemental nations: The Northern and Southern Water Tribes, the Earth Kingdom, the Fire Nation, and the Air Nomads. The Avatar upholds the balance between the nations, but everything changed when the Fire Nation invaded. Only the Avatar, master of all four elements, can stop them. But when the world needs him most, he vanishes. A hundred years later Katara and Sokka discover the new Avatar, an airbender named Aang. Together they must help Aang master the elements and save the world. - Kevin Jeremiah Gaona (IMDB)

Characters

Aang

Aang is already a master airbender and once he learns to manipulate water, earth, and fire, he'll be truly unbeatable.

Katara

In a rag-tag group of kids determined to take down the imperialistic Fire Lord, Katara is the glue that holds them together.

Sokka

Sokka is the group's self proclaimed comedian, occasionally he'll even make people laugh!

Toph

Toph is a tenacious earthbender full of spirit and spunk and hates when people try to baby her because she's blind.

Zuko

The crown prince of the Fire Nation, Zuko has been ordered by his father to find the long-lost Avatar before he is allowed to return home.

Azula

The crown princess of the fire nation and a fire-bending prodigy, Azula was tasked by the fire lord with hunting down the avatar and her traitorous brother, Zuko, as well as her uncle, Iroh.

Mai

Mai is a noble from the Fire Nation and a childhood friend of Princess Azula who specializes in fighting with bladed weapons. She is friends with Azula and Ty Lee.

Ty Lee

Ty Lee is the daughter of a noble from the Fire Nation who ran away at a young age to join the circus. She, along with Azula and Mai are tasked to hunt down the Avatar.

Iroh

Iroh is a former Fire Nation General and brother to Ozai. When Zuko was banished from the Fire Nation, Iroh was tasked to be his guardian while Zuko searched for the Avatar.

Ozai

Ozai is the Firelord and main antagonist of the series. He banished Prince Zuko, his son, until he could find the Avatar and thus restore his honor.
